Who I Am

Data Scientist | Finance Enthusiast | Innovator

Hello, I'm Connor Shen, a data enthusiast transforming complex financial data into actionable insights that drive business decisions. I'm a native of Austin, Texas, with a foundation in Economics from The University of Texas at Austin, where I'm also pursuing a minor in Statistics and Data Science.

What started as a fascination with economic theory evolved into a deep-seated passion for data science and quantitative analysis, leading me to pivot my focus toward the intersection of finance and technology. During my academic journey, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects that showcase the power of data-driven decision making. My work has contributed to significant achievements, including developing trading strategies that outperformed the S&P 500 by over 50%, leading innovative solar energy simulations, and collaborating on NASA's Space Exploration Vehicle project. These experiences across different sectors have sharpened my ability to translate complex data into meaningful insights that drive real-world impact.
